Plasma Passage and Hypernaturals

During June, I'll be releasing a new island called Plasma Passage! This is the first in the series called Hypernaturals. This is an idea I have over half a year ago, and I'm so excited to finally be showing it off So, what are Hypernaturals? Hypernaturals are unstable monstesr hybrids with natural and ethereal elements. Along with them come Ethereal Essences. While they may look like the scrapped natural essences from Dawn of Fire, they have been infused with the ethereal elements due to the ethereals' impact on the mirror timeline. Ethereal Essences can be obtained by searching the island, similar to Crystals. Once you have this essence, you can combine it with natural eggs to infuse it with the etheral element and get a Hypernatural hybrid! I can't wait for you all to discover this island next month.
