These islands work through a new feature: Ethereal Essences! Essences are used to obtain hypernatural monsters. On the surface, they appear to be the natural essences from Dawn of Fire, however the ethereals' instability in the mirror timeline caused these essences to be imbued with the ethereal elements. Similar to crystals in Dawn of Fire, these Essences can be found along the outskirts of the island. Once obtained, you can combine them with a natural monster to create a new Hypernatural hybrid!
