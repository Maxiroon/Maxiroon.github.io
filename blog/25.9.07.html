Heya! The site overhaul continues! Now that all the pages have been overhauled into a more user friendly experience, it's time to get into the fun really specific stuff! I've taken the time to make sure this website is better formatted on mobile. That way images, text, and other elements are big enough to read, but small enough to fit on the screen. Here is a list of most (but not all) changes:

The blog have switched from four cards per row to only two

Images in the About section on the home page will appear above the text as opposed to the left

Text on various pages has changed size for better readability

Cards on all music pages have switched from two cards per row to one

Turned the navigation bar into a dropdown menu
