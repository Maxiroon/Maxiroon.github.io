Maxiroon's Blue Era

Sept 30, 2025

Back

Woah! We're blue now!

I've noticed that Maxi, my OC, is always accompanied by a blue background whenever I commission art for him. So, I figured "What if I changed my channel branding to blue?" After asking a few people I knew what they think, as well as a YouTube poll (which got almost 200 votes), it was pretty obvious that blue perferred over red!

So, taking that into consideration, my branding has changed! My YouTube, Twitter, Discord, Spotify, Kofi, and anywhere else I'm found will now feature blue instead of red.
