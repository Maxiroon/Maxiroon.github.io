About Maxiroon

Hey there, I'm Hailey (or more commonly Maxi)! I am a relatively small YouTuber with about 25,000 subscribers. I make content about My Singing Monsters, however the heart of my channel is music. When I was in middle school, I joined my school's choir. This sparked a huge love for music in me. Since then, I've learnt a few instruments, been in my schools' musicals, learnt how to compose, and constructed a mini music studio. It's currently my goal to work in Musical education, and I can't wait for that dream to come true.

About Maxi

Maxi can refer to me, however it can also refer to my character, Maxi! My online presence has a lot to do with Maxi, as he was originally created to represent me. Nowadays, however, Maxi is a bit more of his own thing, with his own story to follow. While I have yet to explore most of this story with y'all, I have written lots to tell. I hope one day I get to share it all with you!

Here's some info about Maxi: he is a gray cat with lighter spots all over. He has blue and green eyes. He loves music. He also has a sister Lilac who is all white with purple and lilac eyes. They are both pictured to the left.
