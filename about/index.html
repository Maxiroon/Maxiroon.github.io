About Maxiroon (The real person)

Maxiroon is a small YouTuber with around 20,000 subscribers on the platform. He creates content centered around the mobile game My Singing Monsters, including reactions, fan created monsters, fan islands, and more. He may also post something relating to general music. He also has a secondary channel, Maxi2oon, where he occasionally posts random content, usually unedited.


About Maxi (the character)

The Maxiroon brand is centered around Maxiroon's online persona, Maxi. Maxi is a fictional anthropomorphic cat based of Maxiroon's real cat, Max. There is a considerable story behind this character, which has yet to be explored. Maxi also has a sister, Lilac. Both of their official design references can be found below.
