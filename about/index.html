About Maxiroon (The real person)

Maxiroon is a small YouTuber with around 20,000 subscribers on the platform. She (or he) creates content centered around the mobile game My Singing Monsters, including reactions, fan created monsters, fan islands, and more. She may also post something relating to general music. She also has a secondary channel, Maxi2oon, where she occasionally posts random content, usually unedited.

Maxiroon is working to pursue a career in musical education. She uses her YouTube channel as a way to practice and express her musical skills for her future.


About Maxi (the character)

The Maxiroon brand is centered around Maxiroon's online persona, Maxi. Maxi is a fictional anthropomorphic cat based of Maxiroon's real cat, Max.
